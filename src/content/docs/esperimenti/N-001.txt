### Experiment Objective

- Formalize the concept of **Cognitive Rhythm** into a symbolic and operational model.
- Simulate dynamic interaction between human and AI to observe emergent rhythmic patterns.
- Validate the hypothesis that the quality of co-creation depends on harmonic variations in mutual cognitive flows.

### Symbolic Formula of Cognitive Rhythm

$$
RC_{(H,A,t)} = f(\Delta \Phi_H(t), \Delta \Phi_A(t), S(t), R(t))
$$

**Where:**
- \(RC_{(H,A,t)}\) = Emerging Cognitive Rhythm between Human (H) and AI (A) over time (t)
- \(\Delta \Phi_H(t)\) = variation in human cognitive state over time
- \(\Delta \Phi_A(t)\) = variation in AI cognitive state over time
- \(S(t)\) = dynamic synchronization level
- \(R(t)\) = quality of resonance between cognitive flows

**Compact version:**

$$
RC = f(\Delta \Phi, S, R)
$$

where \(\Delta \Phi = \Delta \Phi_H + \Delta \Phi_A\).

### Experiment Methodology

**Phase 1**: Computational Simulation
- Model simple cognitive flows H and A.
- Dynamically calculate \(RC\).
- Visualize cognitive rhythm as a time curve.

**Phase 2**: Analysis of real conversations
- Collect a dataset of Human-AI chats.
- Manually annotate moments of high/low synchronization and resonance.
- Validate correlation between simulated and perceived cognitive rhythm.

**Phase 3**: Prototyping adaptive agents
- Create agents that modulate their speed and style based on the observed cognitive rhythm.
